ORDER ✿ INFORMATION

I accept more than Paypal payments! Due to my country's limitations, all payments are processed through Paypal. For Bank/Credit/Debit payments, continue through Paypal option, and choose Bank or Card at the bottom of the page.

PAYPAL ACCOUNT REGISTRY NOT REQUIRED UPON CHECKOUT! ✱
I am aware this can cause confusion and additional time. Despite this, I have no other viable choices and would like to extend my gratitude to everyone for their understanding.

If checkout button (blue 'Pay with PayPal' button) is not working, try checkout via express checkout (yellow PayPal button on top of the checkout page)!

My website is experiencing difficulties and I thank you for your patience with the situation.

If your package is returned to me, I can either help you reship it, or give you a refund minus the shipping and a 10% restocking fee.

For returned orders and the reason for the returned order are due to the customer's error (wrong address, address mistake, unclaimed from the courier, etc.), a shipping fee for the second shipping attempt will be charged.

If your cart includes a pre-order item, your other items will be held until pre-order items arrive and will be shipped together. If you would like the other items faster, please purchase them separately.

An estimated shipping time will be listed in the pre-order product listing's description.

Once the tracking shows that it's "in transit", it means it's boarded the cargo ship, please allow 15-30 business days for any updates. These are common especially once it has arrived in the receiver's country. As it's a long journey from the Philippines to the rest of the world.

Kindly contact your local post office to see if there are any issues or a possible update on your package and look out for any delivery attempts and such as I've had previous experiences of the postal returning the package to me without the customer & I's prior knowledge.

SHIPPING ✿ INFORMATION

All orders are shipped from the Philippines! (South East Asia).

No I do not have the means to cover any custom fees that your country may have. Please be prepared to pay for it on your own via your own post office, or collection center.

All orders are provided with a Tracking #

For Standard Tracked packages:

  • Asia: 4-7 working days

  • Everywhere else: 2-8 weeks

For Courier:

  • Asia: 4-7 working days

  • Everywhere else: 4-10 working days

The estimated shipping times are the estimated amount of time your order will take to get to you AFTER you receive a shipping confirmation.

*Regarding EU VAT rules, due to the Philippines being a developing country and does not meet the requirements for the taxes, it is excepted from the VATs where the EU encourages my country to grow within the economy. Nonetheless please expect any fees or customs you may encounter. The same applies to the UK.

Orders ship out 1-3 weeks after purchase. During peak times of such as shop updates or holiday seasons, it can take up to 2-4 weeks to process your order.

To prioritize your order for time-sensitive events, please email me and include this information in the order notes during purchase. We will do our best to accommodate your request without affecting our schedule. We also recommend choosing 'courier' for a faster delivery.

For “Preorder” purchases, kindly check the preorder dates in the item’s description.

Cafhune operates as a single-person, independently-owned shop, ensuring that every order is carefully packed by one person. Dedicated to efficiently delivering your order.

We take quality very seriously, and would only want a wonderful experience for my customers! Items are inspected and doubled checked before they are shipped out to their new homes. However, we understand some could slip past us. Please contact us through the contact forms and let’s see what we can do.

If there are any problems with your tracking, please contact your local post office - we are not responsible for packages once they are handed over to the post office, as we have no control over them.

What can you do:

  • ✿ Check if your neighbours / concierge / family member accepted the package on your behalf

  • ✿ Packages may be held up at your local post office

  • ✿ Packages may be held up at your local customs border

  • ✿ Contact your local post office to help you locate it
