ORDER ✿ INFORMATION

I use Procreate on an iPad Pro 10.2"
VLLO app for video editing. Notion and Google Sheets for organizing.

The majority of my products are outsourced and manufactured by small local suppliers here in the Philippines. #SupportLocal

The rest are either handmade by me or manufactured via Alibaba suppliers. I do not disclose further details regarding my suppliers.

I quickly restock my stickers, button pins, art prints, and keychains ASAP. Some products may take longer but most likely will be available again too.

I will not restock items listed as [last chance] or [discontinued]!

I do collaborative merchandise projects friends only. Invited artist for fanzine projects are accepted if it interests me.

For sponsorship collaborations, kindly email me at hi.cafhune@gmail.com or message me on instagram. I accept X deals and cash incentives collaborations.

To resell my official merchandise, it is important to note that it should be presented as a preloved item. Preloved items are those that were originally purchased directly from me but are no longer wanted by the original owner. Ensure that you resell it at a price equal to or lower than the one you initially purchased it for.

However, engaging in unauthorized resale, theft of my designs, or selling them on any other store is strictly prohibited. I will take legal actions.
